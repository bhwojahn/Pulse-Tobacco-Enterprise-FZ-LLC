ABOUT US

Pulse Tobacco Enterprise FZ-LLC, founded in December 2024, is a trusted global supplier of tobacco, connected with the world's most discerning tobacco manufacturers.

Here at Pulse Tobacco we maintain a strict business process, guaranteeing the best tobacco products on the market.

SOURCING

We maintain direct relationships with suppliers across key tobacco-producing regions. Our network spans multiple countries. We prioritize quality, consistency, and ethical sourcing practices.

SAMPLING

Every batch undergoes rigorous quality assessment. We evaluate moisture content, color, texture, and grade classification. Our quality standards align with international specifications.

LOGISTICS

Efficient supply chain management from origin to destination. We handle documentation, compliance, and shipping coordination. Flexible shipment sizes and reliable delivery schedules.

PRODUCTS

Pulse Tobacco is on a mission to provide the best quality products to our clients. We cover a wide range of tobacco products such as Flue Cured Virginia, Air Cured Burley, Dark Air Cured, Dark Fire Cured and Oriental as well as byproducts such as Stems and Fines.
